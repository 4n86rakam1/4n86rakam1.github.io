    INTIGRITI{H1dd3n_Crypt0Cat_Purr}

    Solution

    1. Open the attached otw_pt2.pcapng file with Wireshark
    2. Click on an arbitrary TCP packet
    3. In above menu, Analyze > Follow > TCP Stream
    4. Increments Stream number
    5. In Stream 19, got SMTP packets.
